# Welcome to Carrot Documentation

Carrot consists of two tools: [Carrot Mapper web app](mapper) for generating mapping rules, and
[Carrot Transform ETL tool](transform) for applying mapping rules to data.

At a glance, the diagram below shows the functions of Carrot Mapper (steps 3-5) and Carrot Transform (step 7) in the process of mapping data to OMOP CDM.
